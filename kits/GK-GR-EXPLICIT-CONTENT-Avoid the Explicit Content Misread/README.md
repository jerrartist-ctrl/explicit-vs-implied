People use the word **“explicit”** in different ways. That mismatch can create confusion, rework, or even moderation/compliance mistakes.

This product helps when you need to:
- Disambiguate **“be explicit”** (clarity/specification) vs **“explicit content”** (mature/NSFW labeling).

- Reduce “I thought you meant *that* kind of explicit” moments—fast.
