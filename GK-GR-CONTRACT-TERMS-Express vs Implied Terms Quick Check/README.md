## What problem this solves

When language is vague, hidden assumptions creep in. This product is designed to help you:

- Spot where obligations, scope, or timing are unclear
- Reduce “but we thought…” misunderstandings between parties
- Catch clauses that rely on unstated expectations
- Improve consistency across your internal contract reviews
- Save time during negotiations by focusing attention where risk tends to hide
